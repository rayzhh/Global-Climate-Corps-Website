Welcome.

Mobilizing the wisdom of the elders for a more resilient planet

Climate SAGE addresses two pressing issues, each global and local in nature…

The climate crisis and ageism.

Both are born from our sense of disconnection –
from the natural world and from the inherent value of people of all ages.

We are elder strong in a climate crisis.
