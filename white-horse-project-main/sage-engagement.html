SAGE Engagement

We are seeking adults, age 50 and older, who are:

  • Called to take meaningful action in response to the climate crisis
  • Inspired to share their lived experience in empowering ways
  • Motivated to help redefine perceptions of later life
  • Prepared to reclaim their rightful role as elders in our society.

As Climate SAGEs, elders can experience 4 interconnected, engagement pathways:

SEEDS

For activists-in-the-making. SEEDS includes an online podcast and an engaging, self-study course featuring a whole systems approach to the climate crisis and the need for Climate SAGEs.

CIRCLES

For community-builders. This package combines education and action, by bundling SEEDS access and a turn-key toolkit on how to create Climate SAGE circles, a community-based discussion/action forum that functions at a local, grassroots level.

DELEGATIONS

For bridge builders. Climate justice experiences that bring Climate SAGEs together in dialogue with other elders from climate impacted communities. Delegates experience SEEDS training and travel to either domestic or international delegation sites.

EXPEDITIONS

For adventurers. After completing SEEDS training, Climate SAGES experience meaningful, hands-on, single and multi-day climate action experiences. Members select from issue-specific options (i.e. coral bleaching, water quality, erratic weather, soil management/food production) to advance mitigation or adaptation strategies, either regionally or internationally.
