Who We Are

Laura Beck holds an MPA in Public Affairs, with an emphasis on environmental policy, and a BS in Communications. After serving as an advocate for her father, who lived with Alzheimer’s disease for 10 years, her career took a surprising turn. Having witnessed the crippling effect of ageism and ableism that elders experience, Laura began a professional journey focused on creating empowered cultures for elders and those who support them.

For 18 years now, Laura has worked alongside international aging thought leaders, including award winning author and geriatrician, Dr. Bill Thomas, to help drive revolutionary shifts in how we see, engage, and care for elders. She has also worked in collaboration with other aging specialists on the creation of transformative education designed to drive new paradigms, including how elders and youth can join forces as agents of change.

Through Climate Sage, Laura is honored and thrilled to merge two primary passions – reinventing the role of elders in our communities and creating empowering ways to reclaim the health of our planet.

Nathan Kumar Scott has been a leader in connecting local community engagement with global experiential education for over 20 years. Nathan has directed a variety of organizations: from Cornell Cooperative Extension serving the residents of Schuyler County, NY; to Winterline Global Education in Cambridge, MA, providing youth with international skill building experiences; to SAGE Global Education in Fort Collins, CO, developing global awareness and connection among youth around the world.

Nathan was born and raised in India, growing up in the foothills of the Himalayas. He holds an M.A. from the University of Washington in International Studies and a B.A. from Oberlin College in cultural anthropology. Nathan has been a Watson Fellow, studying non-formal education throughout South and Southeast Asia, as well as a Fulbright Fellow, studying Indian folk performing arts traditions.

Nathan is the co-founder of the youth climate leadership organization, Global Climate Corps. His passion lies in engaging youth and elders together while empowering all communities to address the most pressing issues of our time.
